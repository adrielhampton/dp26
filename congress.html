Where the Fight Is Happening

Races to Watch in 2026

These are the districts where the anti-ICE message is on the ballot and where the outcome will shape whether Congress votes to repeal and replace Trump's Gestapo.

IL-09 — Illinois
Open Seat
Rep. Jan Schakowsky is retiring. Top contenders Kat Abughazaleh and Daniel Biss have both committed to abolishing ICE. Abughazaleh was indicted after blocking an ICE vehicle. This is a safe Democratic seat where the question is how bold the next member will be.
NY-12 — New York
Open Seat
Rep. Jerry Nadler is retiring. Cameron Kasky is running on abolishing ICE — calling it "an organization that can be turned into the President's fascist secret police in a matter of months."
CA-07 — California
Justice Dems Primary
Mai Vang, daughter of Hmong refugees and Sacramento City Councilmember, is challenging Rep. Doris Matsui on an explicit abolish ICE platform. "People are being killed and murdered by ICE. It's not radical to want to dismantle an agency that is killing citizens."
ME-02 — Maine
Battleground
Open seat after Rep. Jared Golden retires. Graham Platner is running hard on ICE accountability — "Organizations that are used to kidnap Americans should not exist." One of the most-watched open seats in the country.
NJ-11 — New Jersey
Battleground
Open seat special election. Progressive Analilia Mejia is running against a DCCC-backed establishment Democrat in a race dominated by ICE funding as an issue. The progressive case is winning.
IL-04 — Illinois
Open Seat
Patty Garcia launched her campaign with an unambiguous message: "Let me be clear: F--- ICE. It's time to abolish ICE and hold Trump and his entire clan accountable." This is what bold looks like.
PA-07 & PA-08 — Pennsylvania
DCCC Targets
Two competitive Pennsylvania seats on the DCCC's target list. Democrats are outperforming their 2024 numbers in Pennsylvania by double digits. These seats flip the House.
AZ-01 & AZ-06 — Arizona
DCCC Targets
Two Arizona battlegrounds where ICE operations have terrorized Latino communities. Both are on the DCCC target list. Arizona is where the ground is shifting fastest.
