About

We're Done Watching.
We're Standing Up.

Democrats.com launched this campaign because the courage of immigrant communities — and the neighbors who stand with them — demands a Congress that acts.

We name the crimes: murder, torture, child abuse. We name who enables them. And we demand they be held accountable.

What We Believe

ICE is not law enforcement. It is Trump's political weapon — built to terrorize communities, fill concentration camps, and enrich his allies.

This isn't a policy disagreement. This is murder, torture, and child abuse. Every member of Congress who funds it owns what happens inside those camps.

What We're Doing

We have one goal: demand Congress repeal and replace ICE. Not negotiate it. Not limit it. Repeal and replace — as many times as it takes.

We are building a powerful army of Americans who won't accept this. We are organizing communities to fight back. We are demanding Congress act.

The Three Pillars

  • Close the Camps. End ICE's murder, torture, and child abuse. Shut down every concentration camp operating on American soil.
  • Defund the Agents. Cut off the funding that keeps Trump's Gestapo operating. No more taxpayer money for concentration camps.
  • Elect a New Congress. Every member who funds ICE's murder and torture must be replaced with one who will vote to repeal and replace.

Our Sponsor

Stop ICE is a campaign sponsored by Democrats.com, one of the oldest and largest progressive online political organizations in America. Democrats.com has been mobilizing Democratic voters and holding politicians accountable since 1999.
